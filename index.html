We are a team of students, researchers and developers at the AGH University of Krakow 🎓 in Poland 🇵🇱. Our activities concern free/libre and open-source software for atmospheric sciences 🌍, and cloud physics 🌧️ in particular. We develop example-rich tools to support reproducible computational science, while promoting best practices in research software engineering. Our team contributes to the open-source ecosystem we rely on. We explore modern, transferable technologies that are useful both within and beyond academia.
